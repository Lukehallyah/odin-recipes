Meatloaf

Delicious Goodness

In this recipe, we will explore the bounds of culinary delights in the form of this delicious meatloaf. Moist and brimming with fresh flavor, this recipe is sure to delight all of your friends and family, before you know it those around you will be asking for this recipe, you'll be a hit at all of your family gatherings!

Easy to prepare and a flavor to bring everyone back for seconds, this recipe is easy to follow and to prepare, all there is to do is to mix the ingredients together carefully and viola! Before you know it a quick and easy meal that is sure to dazzle all those around you.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of ground chuck
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup dry breadcrumbs
  • 1 tablespoon of yellow mustard
  • 1 tablespoon of worchestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of ketchup
  • 2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 cup milk
  • For the topping: 1/2 cup ketchup

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 F
  2. Combine all of the ingredients into a bowl except for the milk and mix thoroughly. Also leave the topping ketchup for later. Finally mix in the milk as a last step, everything should be pretty sticky
  3. Form the meatloaf in a small pan ensuring to pick one that has sides, making a small rectangular meat patty and finally covering the top with the topping ketchup
  4. Bake until an internal temperature of 160 F which should take approximately an hour and a half. The meatloaf should be allowed to rest for 10 minutes or so and cool before serving
