Memories are an amazing thing, bringing us into the sweet embrace of childhood and thoughts of home and family. The smells, the sounds, the taste of an amazing home cooked meal brings joy to the heart and soul, renewing the spirit! In Odin's Recipes, we hope to bring to mind the feeling of family, the loveliness of a warm Meatloaf on a winter's evening, the wafting scents of hickory and honey of a smoked ham, and the sizzling sounds of Porkchops on a lazy spring day. Whatever you're looking for, Odin's recipes has it all, and you can even find out how to make it for yourself!
